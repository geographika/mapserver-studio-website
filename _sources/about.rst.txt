About
=====

MapServer Studio is created by `Geographika Ltd. <https://geographika.net/>`_
a UK based geospatial consultancy. 

If you require MapServer support or development please get in touch at contact@mapserverstudio.net.
You can find Geographika Ltd. listed on the `MapServer Service Providers page <https://mapserver.org/community/service_providers.html>`_.

Privacy
-------

Only the minimal amount of data is stored by MapServer Studio - your user login email, and any Mapfiles
associated with your account. No passwords or payment details are stored on MapServer Studio servers.

+ Password management is handled by https://auth0.com/
+ Payments and credit card details are handled by https://stripe.com
