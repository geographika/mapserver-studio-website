Pricing#

Note

If you have any problems with payments or with paid subscriptions please contact support@mapserverstudio.net

MapServer Studio is currently in its early days of development. Purchasing a subscription brings the following benefits:

  • Support the ongoing and future development of MapServer Studio

  • Support continuing maintenance and feature development of the MapServer project (see contributions to MapServer).
